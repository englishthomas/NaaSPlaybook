L&W Metrics Calculation Widget

This widget calculates the most relevant metrics for Logistics & Warehousing. (i.e. Economic Order Quantity and Reorder Level)

In order to use this widget, perform the following steps:

  • Introduce the characteristics of the Inventory, according to the given units.
  • Click on the "Calculate" button to generate the L&W Metrics.
  • The L&W Metrics can be found in the Results section.

Basics for this widget are addressed on the Logistics & Warehousing Module.
